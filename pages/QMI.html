Quantum Mechanics Interpretation with Nicolas Gisin & Renato Renner

Brief description of the event

The speakers are Renato Renner and Nicolas Gisin. Renato Renner is the professors of ETH Zurich, Quantum Information Theory group. Nicolas Gisin is an Proffessor at University of Geneva and Constructor University. Both scientists are working with the quantum mechanics foundations which is the key point of the current meeting. Two 20 minutes lectures are followed by the live discussion where two different points of view are compared and studied deeper!

Abstract

Do we need a new interpretation and cannot accept any of the currently existed or should we give up determinism staying with the realism? This questions we will try to answer during the life debates after brief lectures!
